## How It Works:

In Aspire applications, the `Projects` namespace is auto-generated by analyzing ProjectReferences:

1. **ProjectReference** in .csproj → `ScimAPI\ScimAPI.csproj`
2. **AssemblyName** → `ScimAPI`
3. **Generated Class** → `Projects.ScimAPI`
4. **Usage** → `builder.AddProject<Projects.ScimAPI>("scimapi")`
